Module_2: Designing

Introduction about Designing

Fusion 360 is a versatile computer-aided design (CAD), computer-aided manufacturing (CAM), and computer-aided engineering (CAE) software developed by Autodesk. It supports both 2D and 3D design, offering a comprehensive platform for product development and engineering.

*3D Design*

Fusion 360 enables users to create detailed 3D models of objects and prototypes. With its intuitive interface and powerful tools, designers can sculpt, sculpt, and manipulate 3D shapes with precision. This allows for the visualization of complex designs from various angles and perspectives, aiding in the development of innovative products and prototypes.

*2D Design*

In addition to 3D modeling, Fusion 360 also supports 2D drafting and design. Users can create 2D sketches and drawings to define the basic geometry and dimensions of their designs. These sketches can then be used as a basis for creating 3D models or for generating technical documentation such as engineering drawings and schematics.
