My Story

I'm Tanmay Kurhekar

My name is Tanmay Anand Kurhekar, and I'm a 19-year-old male from Shegaon, Maharashtra. I completed my 10th grade at St. Xavier English School in Shegaon and my 12th grade at Sau Kamal Dawale Jr. College in Akola. In my free time, I love playing chess and cricket. Whether I'm strategizing on the chessboard or enjoying a game of cricket, these hobbies bring me both relaxation and excitement. I'm a die-hard fan of the Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League, and I cheer for them with passion and enthusiasm during every match. Besides sports, I have a keen interest in watching different web series. Exploring various narratives and characters on screen is something I find truly enjoyable and enriching. Currently, I'm pursuing a degree in electrical engineering at Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering in Shegaon. Alongside my studies, I actively participate in various college events and initiatives. As a member of the Science World Committee, I contribute to spreading scientific awareness and fostering curiosity among my peers. Additionally, I'm involved with Fab Lab, where I engage in hands-on learning and innovation with fellow students. I approach life with humility and respect, eager to learn from every experience and encounter. My dedication to my studies and involvement in extracurricular activities reflect my commitment to personal growth and community engagement. As I continue my journey, I remain grounded in my values, always ready to embrace new challenges and opportunities with humility and grace. My goal is to make a positive impact on my community and contribute meaningfully to the world around me.
